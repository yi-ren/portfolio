User Persona

Name: Alexa

Age: 22

Alexa is a senior at Suffolk University in Boston. Summer is coming and she can’t find her old sunglasses. She’s heard of this website that sells them and decides to give it a try.

Goals

Alexa wants to ind the sunglasses that fit her needs and budget. She doesn't want to waste time but wants to make sure she gets the best option possible.

Scenarios

Scenario 1 - Browsing the Site

Alexa Googles the name of the site, click on the first result and opens the homepage of site.lex She doesn’t know exactly what she wants so she wants to look at what the site offers. She clicks on Women - Bestsellers.

Scenario 2 - Narrowing Down the Search

Alexa doesn’t care that much about the brand, but she does care about the price. She’s on a budget and she thinks a pair of sunglasses that are about $80 would do. So she goes to the price slider and put the range $70 - $90 down.

She sees that you can also filter by the color of the sunglasses. She thinks black goes with anything, so she chooses black.

She doesn’t know the lingos of the shapes of a pair of sunglasses, but she does know that she has an oval face. So she chooses her face shape as oval, and let the site decide what sunglasses would look good on her.

Now that there are only 4 pages of items left, she decides to sort them by price, low to high.
